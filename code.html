ABOUT ME

I'm an experienced Australian software engineer & team lead working on train, tram and vehicle simulators. I currently specialise in programming languages like C++, C#, using tools including Visual Studio, Unity and UE4. I'm also a private company owner and have previously worked as a self-employed software contractor. I've developed and deployed apps to online stores including Steam. I've designed & developed standalone 3D engines and simulation tools, developed client management software & created signal processing tools for data analysis. I've mentored fellow engineers by supporting their learning and career development. I've previously lectured and tutored classes of 20+ programming students. I have decades of experience working as a software developer with small teams and large organisations working in industries such as simulation, defence, security & games.
