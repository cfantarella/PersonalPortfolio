ABOUT ME

I'm an experienced Australian software developer, specialising in C++, C#, using tools including Visual Studio, Unity and UE4. I spend many of my hours as an enthusiastic programming teacher at Academy of Interactive Entertainment. I've successfully deployed 3D Games to online Stores including Steam. In the past, I've designed & developed standalone 3D Engines using open source libraries, developed Client Management software & created Signal Processing Tools for data analysis. I have 20+ years of experience as a Software Developer with small teams and large organisations, and worked in industries including Defence, Security & Games.
